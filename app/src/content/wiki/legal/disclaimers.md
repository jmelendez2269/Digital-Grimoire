## NON-ENDORSEMENT POLICY

**The inclusion of content, systems, or traditions on Prismarium does not constitute endorsement, validation, verification, or scientific confirmation.**

This includes, but is not limited to:

- Esoteric and occult traditions
- Religious and spiritual teachings
- Mystical practices and experiences
- Philosophical systems
- Traditional sciences and cosmologies
- Systems like astrology, numerology, tarot, human design, and sacred geometry
- Contemporary consciousness sciences
- Alternative healing modalities

### Scientific Validation Not Required

**Scientific validation is not a prerequisite for inclusion in the Prismarium Library.**

Many systems we include (such as astrology, human design, and certain spiritual practices) have not been validated by contemporary scientific methods. **We include them anyway** because:

1. They have historical and cultural significance
2. They provide frameworks that people find meaningful for self-understanding
3. They represent important aspects of human attempts to understand reality
4. The question of their "truth" or "validity" is complex and context-dependent

Users should understand that inclusion does not imply we believe these systems are scientifically accurate or universally valid.
