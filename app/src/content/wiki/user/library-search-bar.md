# Library Search & Discovery

## Introduction

The Search Bar is your primary gateway to the Project Parallax Library. It allows you to instantly find books, grimoires, and resources by title or author without navigating through complex menus.

## User Scenarios

* **Finding a specific text**: You know the title of the book you want to read.
* **Exploring an author**: You want to see all available works by a specific author (e.g., "Crowley").
* **Quick Navigation**: You want to jump straight to a resource from the dashboard.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Locate the Search Bar**: It is prominently displayed on your Dashboard and at the top of the Library page.
2. **Start Typing**: Enter at least **2 characters**.
3. **View Suggestions**: As you type, a dropdown will appear with matching titles and authors.
    * *Click a suggestion* to go directly to that book.
4. **Search All**: If you don't see what you want, press **Enter** or click the **Search Icon** to open the full Library view with your search term applied.

> [!TIP]
> **Power User Tip**: If you want to use advanced filters (like Genre or Era), click the "Go to Library" link below the search bar to access the full filtering engine.
