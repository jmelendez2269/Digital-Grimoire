# User Guide: The Workbench

## Overview

The **Workbench** is your maker's space — a dedicated hub for building and running your personal magical practice. Think of it as your craftsman's bench: the place where you design, create, and operate your rituals and custom tarot cards.

Navigate to the Workbench from the top navigation: **EXTRAS → Workbench**, or click the Workbench card on your home page.

---

## The Three Tools

The Workbench has three tabs at the top.

---

### ⚒️ My Rituals

**URL:** `/workbench/rituals`

This is your personal ritual journal. Rituals you design and save are stored here for repeated use and refinement.

**What you can do:**

* **Create a new ritual** — Click **Create New Ritual** in the top right
* **View your ritual list** — All your saved rites displayed as cards
* **Start a ritual** — Click any ritual card to enter the guided ritual player
* **Perform a ritual step-by-step** — The immersive player guides you through each step with optional timers

**When creating a ritual, you define:**

* Title and primary intention
* Moon phase / timing (optional)
* Estimated duration
* Step-by-step instructions (affirmations, actions, meditations, offerings, etc.)

> **Tip:** A simple ritual you perform consistently is more powerful than a complex one you perform once. Use My Rituals to document what works.

---

### ✨ Ritual Machine

**URL:** `/workbench/machine`

The Ritual Machine gives you access to a curated library of **pre-built ritual protocols** drawn from established esoteric traditions. Select a rite and follow the step-by-step guide.

**What you can do:**

* **Browse protocols** — scroll the grid of available rites
* **Search by name or tag** — use the search bar (e.g. search "moon", "fire", "Mercury")
* **Filter by tradition or difficulty** — use the Filter button
* **Begin a ritual** — click any card to enter the guided player

> **Tip:** If you're new to practice, start here before writing your own rituals in My Rituals. The Ritual Machine gives you a proven framework to build confidence.

---

### 🃏 Deck Forge

**URL:** `/workbench/tarot`

The Deck Forge uses AI to help you create **custom tarot cards** based on your own concepts, imagery, and intentions. Each card you generate is unique to your vision.

**What you can do:**

* **Describe a concept** — type an image description, meaning, or theme
* **Generate a card** — the AI produces a unique illustration and interpretation
* **View your generated cards** — scroll down to see your personal card collection

> The Deck Forge is a *creation tool*. If you want to draw cards for a reading, use [The Oracle](/wiki/tarot) instead.

## Navigation Quick Reference

| I want to… | Go to… |
|---|---|
| Design a personal ritual | Workbench → My Rituals → Create New Ritual |
| Perform one of my saved rituals | Workbench → My Rituals → click a ritual |
| Follow a pre-built ritual guide | Workbench → Ritual Machine |
| Create a custom tarot card | Workbench → Deck Forge |
| Pull cards for a reading | EXTRAS → The Oracle |
| Browse pre-built ritual protocols | EXTRAS → Ritual Library |

## Where Did My Old Rituals Go?

If you had rituals saved before the Workbench was introduced, they are still there. The page moved from the old Practitioner section to `/workbench/rituals`. All your data is intact.
