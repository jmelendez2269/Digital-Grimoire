# User Guide: Practitioner Workbench

## Overview

The **Practitioner Workbench** is your personal studio for managing your magical practice. Consider it the digital equivalent of your physical altar or workspace. Here, you can track your rituals, monitor your consistency, and refine your techniques over time.

## Key Features

### 1. Ritual Management

* **Saved Rituals**: Access all the protocols you've generated with the Ritual Machine or written yourself.
* **Active Rituals**: Mark rituals as "Active" if you are currently working with them. These will appear prominently on your dashboard for easy access.
* **Edit & Refine**: Modification is part of the process. As you perform a ritual, you'll learn what works and what doesn't. Edit your saved rituals to reflect these improvements.

### 2. Practice Tracking

* **Log a Session**: After performing a ritual, log it in the Workbench.
* **Notes**: Add notes about your experience. Did you feel focused? Distracted? Did anything unexpected happen?
* **Mood/Energy**: Rate your energy levels before and after the ritual to track its impact on your state.

### 3. Streaks and Consistency

* **Daily Streaks**: The Workbench tracks consecutive days of practice. Building a streak can be a powerful motivator.
* **Consistency Score**: A metric that reflects your dedication over time.

## Why Use the Workbench?

Magic and self-development are disciplines. Like learning an instrument or training a muscle, they require **consistent effort** and **deliberate practice**. The Workbench provides the structure needed to turn occasional dabbling into a serious path of mastery.

> "The difference between a novice and a master is that the master has failed more times than the novice has tried."

Use the Workbench to track your tries, learn from your "failures," and document your successes.
