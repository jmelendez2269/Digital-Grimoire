# User Guide: Courses

## Overview

**Courses** provide structured learning paths through the vast ocean of esoteric knowledge contained in Project Parallax. While the Library invites open exploration, Courses offer a curated journey to master specific traditions, concepts, or practices.

## How it Works

1. **Browse the Catalog**: Visit the **Courses** page to see available learning paths.
2. **Select a Course**: Click on a course card to view its curriculum, difficulty level, and estimated completion time.
3. **Start Learning**: Begin the first module. Each module may consist of:
    * **Readings**: Curated excerpts from the Library.
    * **Video/Audio**: Multimedia content to deepen understanding.
    * **Quizzes**: Knowledge checks to ensure retention.
    * **Practical Exercises**: Rituals or meditations to apply what you've learned.
4. **Track Progress**: Your progress is automatically saved. You can pause and resume at any time.

## Types of Courses

* **Introductory**: "Esotericism 101", "Tarot Basics", "Meditation Fundamentals".
* **Tradition-Specific**: "Hermeticism", "Kabbalah", "Alchemy".
* **Thematic**: "The Hero's Journey", "Shadow Work", "Sacred Geometry".

## Certification

Upon completing certain advanced courses, you may earn a **Digital Badge** or certificate, which will be displayed on your user profile. This signifies your dedication and understanding of the material.
