## Getting Started

When you log in, you will be greeted by your personal dashboard. This view is designed to be your "home base"—a place to start every session.

### 1. Unified Search

**"Find anything, instantly."**
At the top of your dashboard is the **Search Hub**. This is not just a standard search bar—it uses AI to understand your intent.

* **Search the Library**: Find books and documents.
* **Search Concepts**: Look for ideas within the texts.
* **Ask Questions**: Get answers synthesized from the wisdom traditions in the database.

### 2. Core Tools

These are your primary instruments for study and exploration:

* [**📖 Library**](/library)
    Your archive of knowledge. Browse the complete collection of esoteric texts, sacred writings, and historical documents.

* [**📓 Journal**](/journal) (formerly Digital Grimoire)
    Your personal collection of notes, annotations, and insights.
  * *Note: You can customize the name of your journal in your user settings!*

* [**🕸️ Parallax Graph**](/graph)
    See the unseen connections. This visual tool maps how different concepts, authors, and traditions relate to one another in a 3D network.

* [**🎓 Courses**](/courses)
    Structured learning paths. If you want a guided deep-dive into a specific tradition or topic, start here.

* [**✨ Parallax Engine**](/parallax-engine)
    The engine of insight. This advanced AI tool analyzes any text or topic through **7 distinct lenses**:
    1. Scientific
    2. Psychological
    3. Philosophical
    4. Religious/Spiritual
    5. Historical/Anthropological
    6. Symbolic/Occult
    7. Mathematical

### 3. Practitioner Tools ("Extras")

For those who wish to apply wisdom, not just study it:

* [**⚡ Ritual Machine**](/ritual-machine)
    Generate custom ritual protocols based on your specific intent.

* [**🛠️ Workbench**](/practitioner/rituals)
    Manage your ongoing practices and track your ritual success rates.

* [**🃏 Tarot**](/tarot)
    Consult the digital oracle for daily guidance or specific inquiries.

## Tips for Success

* **Personalize**: Ensure you are logged in so the dashboard can display your custom journal name and track your progress.
* **Explore**: Don't be afraid to click the "Extras". The Tarot and Ritual tools are powerful aids for daily practice.
* **Connect**: Use the Parallax Graph to find unexpected links between the books you read in the Library.
