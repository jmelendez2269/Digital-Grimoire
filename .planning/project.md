# Project Parallax (Convergence)

**"Know Your Craft, Practice Your Craft."**

Project Parallax (internally known as Convergence) is a synthesis-driven learning platform designed for individuals who seek to understand themselves, their world, and their universe through the integration of hidden wisdom across all traditions.

## Core Vision

Convergence is not just an archive, but a **Curated Synthesis**. It treats reality as accessible through multiple "lenses" (Scientific, Psychological, Philosophical, etc.), maintaining that truth is found in the convergence of these diverse perspectives.

## Core Components

1. **Convergence School (Learning Software):** Structured educational platform with foundational courses (8-week cycles) and rotating thematic explorations.
2. **The Study Journal:** A private, Notion-like workspace for documentation, passage clipping, and building interconnected knowledge networks using wiki-links.
3. **Curated Library:** A collection of public domain texts selected for their "Convergence Value"—bridging multiple perspectives and traditions.
4. **Ritual Machine (Future):** A practice-oriented feature for ritual inventory, AI-powered correspondences, and documenting experimental craft.
5. **Parallax Engine:** The AI-orchestration layer that processes information through the 7-Lens framework.

## The Seven Lenses

- **Scientific:** Physics, biology, cosmology, empirical evidence.
- **Psychological:** Jungian/Depth psychology, archetypes, cognitive science.
- **Philosophical:** Metaphysics, ethics, epistemology, ontology.
- **Religious/Spiritual:** Comparative theology, mysticism, sacred texts.
- **Historical/Anthropological:** Cultural evolution, mythology, ritual context.
- **Symbolic/Occult:** Correspondences, alchemy, astrology, esotericism.
- **Mathematical:** Sacred geometry, patterns, universal ratios.

## Guiding Principles

1. **Knowing Is Plural:** Reality is accessed through distinct epistemological instruments; no single lens is final.
2. **Synthesis as Discipline:** The ability to hold incompatible truths without collapsing them into false unity.
3. **Questions are Primary:** Every exploration is structured around inquiry, not doctrine.
4. **Epistemic Self-Authority:** Training the capacity to make meaning across multiple realities without dependency on institutional authority.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Next.js (App Router), TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn UI.
- **Backend/Database:** Supabase (Postgres, Vector, Auth).
- **AI:** Vercel AI SDK, Multi-Model Orchestration (OpenAI/Anthropic/Google).
- **Workflow:** GSD (Spec-Driven Development).
