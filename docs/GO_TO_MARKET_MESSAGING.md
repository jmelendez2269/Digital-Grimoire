## Study Journal Features

### Core Messaging

**Headline:** "Document Your Discoveries as You Read"

**Subheadline:** "Your personal research workspace with direct library integration, wiki-links, and auto-save—never lose an insight."

### Feature Descriptions

#### 1. Document Findings as You Read

**Message:** "Capture insights the moment they emerge"

**Details:**
- Take notes while reading library texts
- Auto-save ensures you never lose thoughts
- Instant access to your journal from any library page
- Seamless workflow: read → document → synthesize

**Use Case:** "Reading *The Kybalion* and discover a connection to quantum physics? Document it immediately in your journal without leaving the text."

#### 2. Clip Passages Directly into Journal

**Message:** "Clip passages directly into your journal from library texts"

**Details:**
- Select any text passage and clip it instantly
- Automatic citation and source linking
- Preserves context and page references
- Build your personal quote collection

**Use Case:** "Found a profound passage in *The Bhagavad Gita*? Clip it directly into your journal entry on 'Divine Unity' with full citation."

#### 3. Add Your Own Text and Notes

**Message:** "Build your personal knowledge network"

**Details:**
- Rich text editor with formatting options
- Add your own insights, connections, and analysis
- Mix clipped passages with your own writing
- Create structured research notebooks

**Use Case:** "Combine clipped passages from multiple texts with your own synthesis, creating a comprehensive research document on 'The Nature of Consciousness.'"

#### 4. Wiki-Links for Interconnected Knowledge

**Message:** "Wiki-links create your personal knowledge network"

**Details:**
- Create links between concepts using `[[double brackets]]`
- Build interconnected note systems like Obsidian
- Visual graph view of your knowledge network (future)
- Never lose context between related ideas

**Use Case:** "Link your journal entry on 'Hermetic Principles' to entries on 'Alchemy' and 'Qabalah'—creating a living knowledge network that grows with your research."

#### 5. Auto-Save, Never Lose Your Insights

**Message:** "Auto-save ensures your insights are always preserved"

**Details:**
- Automatic saving every few seconds
- No manual save required
- Version history (future)
- Access your journal from any device

**Use Case:** "Close your browser mid-thought? No problem. Your insights are automatically saved and ready when you return."

#### 6. Export to Multiple Formats

**Message:** "Export your research to Markdown, HTML, PDF, or Notion"

**Details:**
- Export individual pages or entire journal
- Markdown for version control (Git, Obsidian)
- HTML for web publishing
- PDF for printing or sharing
- Notion import for team collaboration

**Use Case:** "Export your research on 'Mystical Traditions' to Markdown, push to GitHub, and share with your academic research group."

## AI Model Strategy

### Core Messaging

**Headline:** "Choose Your Path: Popular Models or Specialized Deep Inquiry"

**Subheadline:** "Access Claude, GPT, and Gemini for familiar AI assistance, or go deeper with our specialized 7-lens Convergence Machine."

### Two-Tier AI Strategy

#### Tier 1: Universal AI Search (Popular Models)

**Message:** "Access to Claude, GPT, Gemini—the most popular AI models"

**What It Offers:**
- Familiar AI models users already know
- General-purpose question answering
- Fast, reliable responses
- No learning curve

**Use Cases:**
- "What does The Kybalion say about the principle of correspondence?"
- "Summarize the main ideas in The Bhagavad Gita"
- "Explain the concept of emptiness in Buddhism"

**Messaging:**
- "Use the AI models you already know and trust"
- "Familiar AI assistance for your research"
- "Access Claude, GPT, and Gemini in one place"

**Target Audience:**
- Users who want familiar AI tools
- General research questions
- Quick answers and summaries

#### Tier 2: The Convergence Machine (Specialized System)

**Message:** "Specialized 7-lens system for deep esoteric inquiry"

**What It Offers:**
- Seven perspectives on every question:
  1. **Scientific** - Physics, biology, cosmology, empirical evidence
  2. **Psychological** - Jungian archetypes, cognitive science, shadow work
  3. **Philosophical** - Metaphysics, ethics, epistemology, ontology
  4. **Religious/Spiritual** - Comparative theology, mysticism, sacred texts
  5. **Historical/Anthropological** - Cultural evolution, mythology, ritual context
  6. **Symbolic/Occult** - Correspondences, alchemy, astrology, esoteric systems
  7. **Mathematical** - Sacred geometry, numerology, patterns, universal ratios

- Adjustable lens weighting (0-100% per lens)
- Deep synthesis across perspectives
- Esoteric-specific reasoning

**Use Cases:**
- "How do different traditions understand the concept of unity?"
- "What does The Emerald Tablet mean from scientific, alchemical, and philosophical perspectives?"
- "Compare the psychological and spiritual aspects of mystical experience"

**Messaging:**
- "Go deeper with our specialized 7-lens system"
- "The Convergence Machine: Curated specifically for esoteric research"
- "See your question through seven different perspectives"

**Target Audience:**
- Deep researchers and scholars
- Practitioners seeking synthesis
- Users exploring cross-tradition connections
