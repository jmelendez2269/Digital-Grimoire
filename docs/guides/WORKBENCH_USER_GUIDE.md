# Workbench & Extras — End User Guide

**Version:** 1.0  
**Updated:** March 3, 2026

---

## What Changed?

We reorganized the app's "Extras" area to make a clearer distinction between **creating** things and **using** things.

**Old experience:** Creation and consumption were mixed in the same places — confusing!  
**New experience:** The **Workbench** is where you *make*. The **Extras** portals are where you *use*.

---

## The Workbench ⚒️

> **What it is:** Your maker's bench — a dedicated space for building and running your personal magical practice.

**How to get there:** Click `EXTRAS` in the top navigation → select **Workbench**, or click the Workbench card on the home page.

The Workbench has three tabs:

---

### My Rituals 📜

**URL:** `/workbench/rituals`

Design, store, and perform your own personal rituals.

| Action | How To |
|---|---|
| Create a ritual | Click **Create New Ritual** (top right) |
| View a ritual | Click any ritual card |
| Perform a ritual | Click a ritual card → **Begin Ritual** |
| Exit a ritual early | Click the ✕ in the top right during performance |

**When you create a ritual, you define:**

- Title and intention
- Moon phase / timing
- Estimated duration
- Step-by-step instructions (affirmations, meditations, actions, etc.)

---

### Ritual Machine ✨

**URL:** `/workbench/machine`

Browse a curated library of **pre-built ritual protocols** drawn from esoteric traditions. Find a rite, click it, and follow along step by step.

| Action | How To |
|---|---|
| Browse protocols | Scroll the grid |
| Search by name or tag | Use the search bar at the top |
| Filter by tradition | Click the **Filter** button |
| Start a ritual | Click any card to open the ritual |

> **Tip:** The Ritual Machine is perfect if you're new to practice or want a structured starting point before you begin writing your own rites in My Rituals.

---

### Deck Forge 🃏

**URL:** `/workbench/tarot`

Use AI to generate **custom tarot cards** unique to your vision. Give the AI a concept, meaning, or imagery description and it will produce an original card for your personal digital deck.

| Action | How To |
|---|---|
| Generate a card | Describe the concept you want visualized |
| View generated cards | Scroll down to your generated card history |

> Deck Forge is a creation tool — it is separate from The Oracle (which is for reading/drawing only).

---

## The Oracle 🔮

**URL:** `/extras/tarot`

> **What it is:** A quiet, meditative space for pulling tarot cards and receiving guidance.

This page is intentionally simple — no creation tools here, just the draw.

| Action | How To |
|---|---|
| Perform a daily draw | Click **Daily Draw** |
| View past readings | Scroll to the Draw History section |

---

## Ritual Library 📚

**URL:** `/ritual-machine`

Browse the library of curated ritual protocols. This is the consumption-side companion to the Ritual Machine in the Workbench — ideal for exploring what's available before starting a practice.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Where did my old rituals go?**  
A: They're still there! The page moved from `/practitioner/rituals` to `/workbench/rituals`. Your data was not changed.

**Q: I bookmarked the old Deck Forge URL — does it still work?**  
A: Yes. The old `/extras/tarot/forge` URL automatically redirects to `/workbench/tarot`.

**Q: What is the difference between the Ritual Machine (Workbench) and the Ritual Library (Extras)?**  
A: They currently contain the same protocols. The **Ritual Machine** tab in the Workbench is the focused, tool-oriented experience. The **Ritual Library** in Extras is a browsing-first discovery experience. As the app grows, the Workbench version may gain additional features like favorites, progress tracking, and custom modifications.

**Q: Can I create my own tarot deck?**  
A: You can generate individual cards in Deck Forge. A full custom deck management gallery is planned for a future update.
