# Convergence — Social Media Content Strategy

**Where Hidden Wisdom Reveals Our Unity**

This document covers the full content strategy for Convergence: the live show format, platform playbooks, repurposing pipeline, conversion funnel, and phased launch sequence.

---

## Core Principle

Your 90-minute weekly Twitch stream is the "ore." Everything else is refined from it. One recording session yields 35–45 pieces of content across five platforms. You are not creating more content — you are extracting more value from the same two hours.

## The Live Show Format (Twitch — 90 min)

Your weekly show is a live study session, not a lecture. The hook is watching wisdom traditions get cross-examined in real time through the Convergence Machine.

### Stream Rundown

| Segment | Duration | What Happens |
|---------|----------|--------------|
| Cold open / arrival | 5 min | Starting Soon screen, chat banter, pin the week's `core_question` |
| Framing the tension | 10 min | Read the week's `key_tension` aloud, run a chat poll on which side they lean |
| Reading walkthrough | 20 min | Pull course readings from the library on screen, annotate live |
| **The Lens Session** | 30 min | Run the `core_question` through the Convergence Machine live — start balanced, then dramatically shift sliders and re-run; take a chat-suggested question |
| Synthesis with chat | 15 min | Hit the `synthesis_prompt`, read live chat responses on air, synthesize |
| Wrap + funnel | 10 min | Recap, tease next week's tension, point to free course signup |

### The Lens Session — Your Headline Act

This is the spectacle unique to Convergence. No other creator can do this.

1. Take the week's `core_question`
2. Run it at balanced lens weights
3. Dramatically crank one slider (e.g. Symbolic to 100%, Scientific to 0%) and re-run
4. The contrast between outputs is the content
5. Take a viewer-suggested question and do it live

**Read the output out loud and react.** Never let the audience silently read a screen. Narrate the synthesis: *"The Mathematical lens just landed in almost the same place as the Symbolic lens — chat, did you catch that overlap?"*

### OBS Scene Setup

| Scene | Use |
|-------|-----|
| Starting Soon / BRB | Branded dark-academia overlay, music |
| Talking Head | Webcam only, for framing and discussion |
| Screen + Cam | Full-screen app, webcam in corner — 70% of the show |
| Lens Focus Card | Full-screen interstitial announcing each lens — doubles as an editing chapter marker |

The Lens Focus Card is critical. Every time it appears on stream, it creates a clean chapter boundary in your YouTube edit.

### Technical Setup

- **VOD Storage:** Twitch Dashboard → Settings → Stream → Store past broadcasts (enable immediately)
- **Latency:** Use Low Latency mode for responsive chat Q&A
- **Multistreaming:** Use [Restream.io](https://restream.io) (~$16/mo) to broadcast simultaneously to Twitch + Facebook Live + YouTube Live — one stream, three audiences, zero extra effort
- **Pre-stream test:** Queue your exact Convergence Machine queries and run them before going live. An erroring AI mid-stream is your biggest risk.

## Platform Playbooks

### YouTube — Your Evergreen Archive

YouTube is where discovery compounds over time via search. Your dark-academia esoteric content has strong SEO upside: long-tail queries like "Hermeticism explained", "Buddhist emptiness vs quantum physics", "7 lenses of consciousness" have low competition and high intent.

**Three content tiers from one stream:**

1. **Full episode** (60–70 min, edited) — Cut the intro waiting screen and technical difficulties. Heavy use of YouTube Chapters (one per Lens Focus Card = automatic). Titles: *"The Void Through 7 Lenses — Convergence Course: Week 3"*

2. **Modular lessons** (10–20 min) — Each `lens_exercise` or reading walkthrough becomes a standalone lesson. SEO titles: *"What Buddhism, Quantum Physics, and Hermeticism Agree On About Emptiness"*

3. **Shorts** (30–60s) — The sharpest lens contrast moments from the Lens Session. End card + description link to the relevant course.

**Every video ends with:** *"This is Week [N] of the [Course Name] course — start it free at [link]."*

---

### TikTok — Your Discovery Engine

The esoteric/spiritual niche is one of TikTok's most active communities. #WitchTok, #SpiritualTok, #Occult, #Esoteric collectively rack up billions of views. Your content angle — showing AI reconcile ancient traditions in real time — has no direct competition here.

**Best performing formats for your niche:**

| Format | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| The Lens Contrast | *"I asked the AI what 'the void' means — the Scientific and Mystical lenses said basically the same thing"* — screen record the Convergence Machine output, voiceover, on-screen text highlights overlapping phrases |
| The Hot Claim | *"Buddhism and Quantum Physics are the same tradition separated by 2,500 years"* — 45 seconds, fast cuts between lens outputs, end on the synthesis quote |
| The Text Reveal | Film reading from a library text, hit a striking passage, pause — *"this was written in 1580 and describes neuroplasticity exactly"* |
| The Slider Experiment | Screen record: run a question at default weights, slam Symbolic to 100%, re-run — the change is the content |

**Captions:** Non-negotiable. Use CapCut auto-captions on every clip before posting.

**Hashtags:** `#Esoteric #Occult #WitchTok #SpiritualTok #AncientWisdom #PhilosophyTok #Mysticism #HermeticTradition #Consciousness #SacredGeometry`

**Cadence:** 1–2 posts/day. Your weekly clip batch gives you a 10-day buffer.

---

### Instagram — Your Brand Anchor

Instagram skews slightly older and more aesthetics-driven than TikTok. Your Dark Academia visual identity (deep greens, brass/gold accents, parchment textures) is built for Instagram. This is where brand recognition compounds.

**Three content pillars:**

**1. Reels** — Same clips as TikTok. Re-caption them: TikTok captions are punchy/short; Instagram captions can be a thoughtful paragraph. Both audiences exist.

**2. Carousels** — Your highest-engagement format. Design in Canva using dark-academia templates. Ideas:
- *"The 7 Lenses of Convergence"* — one slide per lens, what it does, example output sentence
- *"Week [N] Core Question: [question] — here's how 3 traditions answer it"* (pulled directly from course content, zero extra work)
- *"Hermetic Principles vs. Quantum Mechanics: A Side-by-Side"* — quote card comparison

**3. Quote cards** — Single image posts. Take the most striking sentence from any lens output, format it in Canva. 4 minutes each. Quote cards get saved and shared more than anything else in the spiritual niche.

**Stories:**
- Polls that seed the next stream (*"Which should we run through the Machine this week: Emptiness, The Void, or The Abyss?"*)
- Behind-the-scenes stream prep
- Countdown to Twitch live

**Cadence:** 1 feed post/day (rotate Reels → carousel → quote card), Stories daily.

---

### Facebook — Your Older Audience and Community Hub

Facebook demographics skew 35–65 — a core Convergence buyer (established income, serious spiritual seekers, willing to pay $15/mo). Don't underestimate this platform.

**Two high-leverage moves:**

**1. Native video uploads on your Page** — Do not just link to YouTube. Upload modular lessons directly to Facebook. Native video gets 3–10x the organic reach of an external link in the feed.

**2. Facebook Group: "Convergence Study Circle"** — This is your highest-leverage long-term asset:
- Free to join
- Post the week's `synthesis_prompt` as a discussion question
- Community answers seed your next stream's live chat
- An active group converts visitors to paid subscribers faster than any ad
- Pinned post: free signup link to the Reader tier

**Cadence:** 3–4 native video posts/week on the Page, 1–2 discussion posts in the Group.

This is the compounding advantage no competitor has: **Convergence Machine outputs are your captions, quote cards, and carousel slides.**

Before each stream, run 3–5 questions through the Convergence Machine. Screenshot the most striking sentence from each lens output. Paste into Canva. Post. Your AI builds your content library in real time.

## Phased Launch Sequence

Activate one platform layer at a time. Never add a platform until the previous one is a habit, not a struggle.

### Phase 0 — Setup Week (before first stream)

- Claim handles across Twitch, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook (even platforms you won't touch for a month)
- Build and test OBS scenes: Starting Soon, Talking Head, Screen+Cam, Lens Focus Card
- Run one full private test stream (20 min) — confirm capture, audio, latency
- Enable Twitch VOD storage
- **Do not post publicly yet**

---

### Phase 1 — Weeks 1–2: Core Loop (Twitch + YouTube only)

**Goal:** Make stream → VOD → YouTube episode feel routine. This is engine-building, not audience-building.

- Go live once per week (90-min format)
- Download VOD, edit full episode, upload to YouTube with chapters
- **Gate to next phase:** Can you go from "stream ends" to "YouTube uploaded" in under a day without it being painful?

You'll have 2 streams and 2 episodes banked. Small audience is correct — you're proving the machine works.

---

### Phase 2 — Weeks 3–4: Discovery Layer (TikTok + Instagram Reels)

**Goal:** Start extracting clips and publishing daily short-form content.

- On edit day (Thursday), pull 5–8 short moments from each stream
- Add captions (CapCut auto-captions)
- Post 1/day on TikTok + 1/day on Instagram Reels
- Your Phase 1 backlog gives you a buffer — you're never posting same-day-panicked
- **Gate to next phase:** Clipping is a 90-minute Thursday routine, not a weekly ordeal

This is where first real audience growth appears. TikTok is the fastest discovery surface for the esoteric niche.

---

### Phase 3 — Weeks 5–6: Brand Layer (Instagram feed + Facebook multistream)

**Goal:** Add brand-building content and expand the live audience.

- Launch Instagram carousels + quote cards (3–4/week via Canva templates)
- Begin daily Instagram Stories (polls, countdowns, behind-the-scenes)
- Set up Restream multistreaming — your weekly live now hits Facebook Live simultaneously
- Begin uploading modular lessons natively to Facebook Page
- **Gate to next phase:** Posting to four platforms without scrambling; templates doing the work

---

### Phase 4 — Weeks 7–8: Community + Full Cadence

**Goal:** Activate the high-retention, high-conversion community layer.

- Launch Facebook Group ("Convergence Study Circle")
- Seed with existing followers; post first synthesis prompt discussion
- Set up Buffer or Later for scheduled auto-publishing
- Lock in the full weekly schedule

By Week 8 you are running all five platforms. Each was absorbed as a habit. Most of the week's content flows from one Wednesday stream + one Thursday edit session.
